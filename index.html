How Our Fabrics Are Made

From raw materials to premium textiles - our meticulous manufacturing process

Step 1: Raw Material Selection

We source the finest raw materials including cotton bolls, silk cocoons, flax plants for linen, and premium synthetic fibers. Each material is carefully inspected for quality, purity, and sustainability before entering our production process.

  • 100% natural cotton sourcing
  • Premium silk cocoons selection
  • Organic flax harvesting
  • Quality certification checks

Step 2: Cleaning & Processing

Raw materials undergo thorough cleaning to remove impurities. Cotton is ginned to separate fibers, silk cocoons are boiled to extract continuous threads, and flax is retted to separate usable fibers. This ensures pure, clean fabric base materials.

  • Impurity removal process
  • Fiber separation techniques
  • Natural cleaning methods
  • Purity testing & verification

Step 3: Spinning & Yarn Creation

Cleaned fibers are spun into strong, uniform yarns using advanced spinning technology. The fibers are drawn out, twisted, and wound onto bobbins to create yarn of consistent thickness and strength. This step determines the fabric's durability and texture.

  • Precision spinning technology
  • Consistent yarn thickness
  • Strength testing protocols
  • Multiple yarn grades available

Step 4: Weaving & Fabric Formation

Yarns are woven into fabric using traditional and modern weaving techniques. Our master weavers create various patterns including plain weave, twill, satin, and custom designs. The weaving process determines the fabric's drape, breathability, and appearance.

  • Traditional handloom weaving
  • Modern power loom technology
  • Multiple weave patterns
  • Custom pattern creation

Step 5: Dyeing & Printing

The woven fabric is dyed using premium, color-fast dyes that resist fading. For printed fabrics like our Ankara collection, intricate patterns are applied using screen printing, digital printing, or traditional wax-resist techniques. Colors are vibrant and long-lasting.

  • Eco-friendly dye processes
  • Color-fast guarantee
  • Digital & screen printing
  • Traditional African wax printing

Step 6: Finishing & Quality Control

Final finishing treatments enhance the fabric's properties - softening, mercerizing, or adding special textures. Every bolt undergoes rigorous quality inspection for color consistency, weave integrity, and overall excellence before being prepared for sale.

  • Softening & texturizing
  • Multi-point quality inspection
  • Fabric strength testing
  • Final color matching verification
